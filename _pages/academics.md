## Thesis Work

**Master's Thesis (In Progress):**  
*Dark Matter and Collider Phenomenology in Triplet Extensions of Standard Model*  
Supervisor: Dr. Rahul Srivastava, IISER Bhopal  
Expected completion: May 2025

This thesis investigates scalar and fermion dark matter candidates in SU(2)_L triplet extensions with hypercharge Y=2, analyzing relic density, detection prospects, and collider signatures using state-of-the-art BSM tools.
